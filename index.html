Pondering corner

All of those things make you wonder: why? How is that possible? What makes an individual so indecisive? There are a couple of things to consider here:

  • Fear of failure - I hate not being the best at things when I start them
  • Lack of time - allegedly, but I know I waste a lot of it
  • Overwhelm from too many options
  • Perfectionism
  • Fear of judgment and SHAME - seriously, look up some Brene Brown stuff online, she changed my life in a way
  • Imposter syndrome! Feeling not good enough in something to call it my "hobby" - that conects with SHAME, too. You know the famous question for people wearing a band's shirt? I feel that way toward myself and my interests: not knowledgable enough to be in the club. I remember meeting my ex's parents, and his father asking me: "Well, so what are your hobbies? What do you do?". I was so afraid of saying something that I'm not a master of and feeling judged by them later, than I choose to say "nothing" - as it was ever a better option.
  • Boredom and routine. Things quickly lose their charm and become mundane. Activities that were once pursing my dream start feeling like an unpleasant chore after a week or two.
